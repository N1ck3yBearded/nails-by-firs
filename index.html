The Firs philosophy

Beauty begins where care is uncompromising.

Firs is the evergreen — steady through every season. It is also our name, and our standard. Every appointment moves at the pace of precision: instruments sterilised to clinical protocol, products chosen for nail health before colour, and hands that have shaped thousands of edges to a single, exacting line.

We don't believe luxury is marble and gold. It is the certainty that every file is single-use, every surface is prepared for you alone, and every detail — from cuticle to finish — has been considered twice.

The evergreen standard · Richmond Hill, Toronto

Hygiene first — always

The standards behind every appointment.

01

Clinical sterilisation

Every metal instrument is cleaned, packed and sterilised between clients to medical protocol. Sealed pouches are opened in front of you — proof, not promise.

02

Single-use everything

Files, buffers and abrasives are used once and discarded. Your appointment begins on surfaces prepared for you alone.

03

Premium products

Professional systems selected for nail health first and colour second — gentle on the natural plate, exceptional in wear and finish.

04

Private, unhurried time

One client, one master, full attention. Appointments are paced for precision, never for turnover.

05

Honest consultation

If your nails need rest instead of colour, we will say so. Long-term health is the brief behind every service we recommend.
